PhD. Msc. Arch.

Christian is an architect, urban planner, and also a PhD candidate at the Leibniz University of Hannover. His focus is on the resilience of territory and cities in the face of climate change and sea-level rise. Christian came to Germany in 2012 after spending his whole life in Ecuador. From 2012 to 2018, He completed his studies in architecture and urbanism. The first episode took place at the FH Bielefeld, where he achieved his bachelor's in architecture (BA) with impeccable results, which was accredited by the BDA Masters Award in 2016. The second episode took place at the Leibniz University of Hannover, concluding with an outstanding urban and architectural work (MsC.) entitled "Super Block Cuenca". The period of 2018 - 2022 was a period of return and reunion in his homeland. He had the opportunity to work in the prestigious architecture office of Duran & Hermida architects in Cuenca. In 2019, remembering his father's work, Christian created Proyectual Studio and materialised his first housing and commercial projects in Cuenca.
